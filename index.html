Established 2008 · Kampala, Uganda

Built here.
Operating everywhere.

Uganda's market cannot be reached from a boardroom. You have to be in it. For fifteen years, we have been.

Market Bolt was founded by marketing professionals who believed Ugandan brands deserved world-class activation. We started with conviction, a handful of clients, and fifteen years of combined industry experience.

Today we operate countrywide, with regional reach across East Africa and partnerships with international institutions. From field teams in Gulu to digital platforms in Kampala. The brands that took a chance on us in 2008 are still calling. That is not luck. That is delivery.
